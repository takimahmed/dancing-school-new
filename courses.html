So funktioniert ein Kurs

Ein Curriculum,
fünf Säulen.

Every course — Beginner to Advanced — runs on the same five pillars. You see them every class, every week, in every style. It's the spine of Easy Dance.

I.

Progressive structure

Eight to ten weeks of clear, sequenced lessons. Each class builds on the last. Skip and you're not lost — but you'll feel the difference.

II.

Rotation practice

You dance with many partners every class. Better technique, faster, and you arrive at Friday Social already knowing half the floor.

III.

Personal feedback

Small groups mean we see your dance. Notes are specific — your shoulder, your hip, your timing — not generalized.

IV.

Musicality woven in

You learn to hear the song, not just count the bar. Breaks, silences, instruments — they all become cues you ride.

V.

Friday Social included

Every course comes with weekly entry to our Friday Social. The room where everything you learned in class becomes a dance.

Was ist der Unterschied zwischen Kurs und Drop-in?
Courses are eight-to-ten-week blocks taught in sequence — you grow alongside the same group, building on every previous class. Drop-ins are single classes you can grab any week. Most regulars start with a course; visitors and travelers drop in.
Muss ich mich für alle 8 Wochen verpflichten?
Most students do because that's where the real progress lives. If you miss a class, you can make it up on the same level any other day in the week. We also accept late entry for Beginner courses up to week three.
Mit welchem Kurs soll ich anfangen?
If you've never danced Latin, any of our four "Foundations" courses — Bachata, Salsa, Zouk, or Kizomba. Bachata is the most popular first step. Tell us what you've heard and danced to most; we'll point you to the right room.
Ich habe etwas Erfahrung. Welches Niveau passt?
Book a free trial in the level above where you think you sit. We'll tell you honestly — most dancers slot one tier lower than they expect, and that's where the technique repair happens.
Wie groß ist die maximale Klassengröße?
Twenty-four dancers, capped firmly. Most weeks hover around eighteen. We turn away registrations once a course fills — we'd rather lose the booking than crowd the floor.
Kann ich als Studio-Mitglied jeden Kurs besuchen?
Yes — every group course on this page is included in the €129/month membership, with no booking cap. Privates and weekend workshops are separate.
Was, wenn ich mich anmelde und pausieren muss?
Life happens — travel, injury, exam season. Message us and we'll pause your course block to the next available intake at no charge. We don't believe in penalties for living.
