This is the placeholder privacy policy for Easy Dance Augsburg (Eduardo Millán & Natalia Hríbová GbR, Haunstetter Str. 49, 86161 Augsburg). It explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG).

1. Who we are

[TBD — controller name, address, contact details, DPO if applicable]

We are the data controller for any personal data we collect through this website, in-person at the studio, and through our communication channels (email, WhatsApp, phone). For any questions about your data, contact us at privacy@easy-dance.de.

2. What data we collect

We collect only what we need to run the studio:

  • Identification data: name, email address, phone number, date of birth (for student discount verification).
  • Booking and payment data: classes booked, course block enrolment, payment records.
  • Communication: emails, WhatsApp messages, contact-form submissions.
  • Health information: only if you voluntarily disclose it (injuries, conditions affecting movement).
  • Website analytics: page views and basic device info via a privacy-respecting analytics provider. [TBD — confirm provider]

3. Legal basis

Under Art. 6 GDPR, our legal bases for processing your data are: contract performance (for bookings, memberships, payments), legitimate interest (for studio communications, safety records), consent (for marketing emails, photos in promotional material), and legal obligation (for tax records).

4. How we use your data

  • To process your bookings, course enrolment, and payments.
  • To communicate about classes, cancellations, and studio updates.
  • To send newsletters and event invitations (only if you opt in).
  • To comply with German tax and bookkeeping obligations.
  • To improve our website and services (anonymized analytics).

5. Data we share

We do not sell your data. We share it only with: payment processors (Stripe — for card and bank transfers), email service providers ([TBD — e.g. ConvertKit, Brevo]), and our accountant for legally required tax records. All providers operate within the EU or under equivalent GDPR safeguards.

6. How long we keep your data

Booking records: until the end of our legal retention period (10 years for tax records under German law). Marketing-list data: until you unsubscribe. Health disclosures: deleted within 30 days of your last class. Inquiry-form submissions: 18 months.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to: (a) access the data we hold about you, (b) correct inaccurate data, (c) request deletion ("right to be forgotten") subject to legal retention obligations, (d) object to processing for marketing purposes, (e) restrict processing, (f) data portability, and (g) lodge a complaint with the data protection authority (Hessischer Beauftragter für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit).

Exercise any of these rights by emailing privacy@easy-dance.de. We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies & tracking

[TBD — cookie list, consent banner mechanism, opt-out method]

This website uses essential cookies only (for the language toggle and accessibility preferences). Analytics cookies are loaded only after you opt in via the cookie banner.

9. Photos & video

We sometimes photograph classes and events for use on this website and our social media. We always ask permission of every dancer in frame before taking the photo, and we'll remove any image from our materials within seven days of a written request to privacy@easy-dance.de.

10. Changes to this policy

We'll update this page if our practices change. Material changes will be communicated by email to registered members at least 30 days in advance.
