Frag uns alles →
Wie kündige ich meine Mitgliedschaft?
Email or WhatsApp us. There's no online cancellation form because we want to know why — sometimes we can fix it (different style, different tempo, instructor change). If you still want to cancel, we cancel that day, no notice period.
Kann ich meine Mitgliedschaft pausieren?
Yes. Travel, injury, exam season, anything. Tell us and we'll pause indefinitely. Time paused doesn't count against your subscription. Reactivate the same way.
Ist die Probestunde wirklich kostenlos?
Fully free, no card required. One trial class per person, on any group class on the schedule. Just register so we know to expect you.
Wie sieht es mit Erstattungen aus?
For drop-ins: full refund up to two hours before class. For monthly membership: pro-rated refund for unused days if you cancel mid-month for a refundable reason (illness, relocation). For workshops: refundable up to 7 days prior, after that we'll credit you toward another date.
Kann ich mitten im Kurs den Stil wechseln?
If you're a Member, yes — drop in on any style any week. If you're on a course block, we'll switch you to the matching block in another style for the remaining weeks. Easy.
Bietet ihr Ratenzahlung an?
For workshops over €150 and the corporate blocks, yes — two-installment plans available. Membership is monthly already so no plan needed. Ask us if anything feels stretched.
Sind Kursblöcke in der Mitgliedschaft enthalten?
Yes. Every group course on the catalog is included in the €129 monthly membership. Private lessons and weekend workshops are billed separately (with the 10% member discount).
